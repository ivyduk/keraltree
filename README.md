## Gamified Learning Platform for Healthcare Professionals

KeralTree is an innovative mobile application designed to revolutionize how health organizations monitor and enhance professional development through gamification. By transforming study activities into engaging, rewarding experiences, KeralTree helps healthcare professionals stay motivated and track their continuous learning journey.

## Overview

In the fast-paced world of healthcare, continuous education is critical. KeralTree addresses the challenge of maintaining consistent study habits by applying proven gamification principles to professional development. The platform creates an engaging environment where healthcare workers can track their learning progress, compete with peers, and celebrate achievements.
